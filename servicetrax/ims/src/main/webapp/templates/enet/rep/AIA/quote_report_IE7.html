Performance Expectations

This section outlines the performance expectations Customers have of AIA.

Timeframes

Service Level Targets

  1. Job Completion - 99% target for on-time job completion
  2. Punch List Completion - within 5 days of receipt of punch list product
  3. Invoicing - within 3 days of job completion

Before Commencing Work

Prior to beginning the quoted work, AIA will:
  • Attend any necessary meetings to define the scope of services and any constraints on the project
  • Conduct a physical survey of the site(s) to determine field conditions
  • Comply with all applicable worker's compensation, employer's liability, auto insurance, and other applicable federal, state, community and municipal laws, ordinances, rules, regulations and codes
  • Obtain permits or licenses and make all necessary arrangements with local and state agencies relative to blocking streets, directing traffic, authorizing moving routes and loading points, etc., in order to ensure safe and efficient moves; any city ordinance violations that occur will be the sole responsibility of AIA and its' service providers
  • Provide list of staff who will require access to premises for the duration of the project
  • Provide adequate supervision during work
  • Notify customers, if applicable, within 24 hours of receipt of product, noting any exceptions
  • Confirm receipt and understanding of any installation drawings
During the Work

During the project, AIA will:
  • Only use the appropriate qualified labor
  • Comply with all security rules and procedures established by building management
  • Obtain written approval for labor costs above and beyond original project scope through Change Order process.
  • Furnish all protective materials necessary to prevent damage to floors, walls, ceilings, carpets, windows, furniture, elevators, stairs, equipment, etc. in both new and existing spaces
  • Provide problem management/escalation oversight
  • Provide updates as necessary on job status

At Completion

At the completion of the work, AIA and/or its' subcontractors will:
  • Remove and dispose of all materials such as crates, packing, protective materials; clean up debris created during installation
  • Bring to the attention of Customer or End User any noticeable damage or malfunction of all items which are being moved/installed. Digital pictures are to be taken of all damaged/malfunctioned product.
  • Develop punch list for any outstanding items and ensure plan is in place to resolve open items.
  • Perform punch list labor and validate completion
  • Provide redlined as built drawings as required of all changes and additions made upon completion of project
  • Obtain authorization by assigned project manager for all work; provide invoices, which indicate the project number and Purchase Order number
